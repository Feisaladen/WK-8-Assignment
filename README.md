## üìù Section 1: Short Answer Questions

### 1. Compare and contrast LangChain and AutoGen
LangChain focuses on **orchestrating language models** for tasks like chatbots and multi-step reasoning, offering prompt management, memory handling, and data integration. AutoGen emphasizes **collaborative AI agents**, automating agent-to-agent communication for complex goals. LangChain is developer-centric, ideal for structured workflows, but requires careful prompt design. AutoGen is agent-centric, suitable for multi-agent collaboration, but can be computationally intensive. Both frameworks support modular AI development with different focuses.

### 2. AI Agents in Supply Chain Management
AI Agents optimize supply chains by **predicting demand, automating inventory, and monitoring machinery**. DHL uses AI to forecast parcel volumes and reroute shipments dynamically, while Walmart leverages AI for shelf stocking and replenishment. Agents reduce errors, improve efficiency, and accelerate decision-making, creating agile and resilient supply chains.

### 3. Human-Agent Symbiosis
Human-Agent Symbiosis is **collaborative work between humans and AI**, augmenting human capabilities rather than replacing them. Unlike traditional automation, AI assists decision-making while humans retain oversight. In manufacturing, agents can recommend machine settings while humans approve final decisions. This enhances productivity, adaptability, and strategic focus.

### 4. Ethical Implications in Finance
Autonomous AI Agents in finance pose risks of **bias, lack of transparency, and accountability issues**. Safeguards include explainable AI, human-in-the-loop approval, continuous auditing, and regulatory compliance to prevent unethical or high-risk decisions.

### 5. Memory and State Management
Memory enables AI Agents to **retain context, learn from past interactions, and maintain continuity**. Challenges include efficient storage, consistency, and conflict resolution. Proper memory management is critical for reliability in applications like customer support or industrial control.
